## What Storelli makes

Storelli makes protective gear and apparel for soccer goalkeepers. Core
products: BodyShield / CoolCore leggings, the ExoShield head guard, GK
gloves, sliders, and other protection gear.

## Core positioning

Protection, confidence, injury prevention, turf-burn prevention, durability,
performance edge.

## Main audiences (ICPs)

Parents, Aspiring Pro, Adult Amateur, and the general goalkeeper audience.

## Content goal

Make goalkeeper protection feel urgent, credible, useful, and visually
obvious. Good content shows the problem, proves the protection, and makes
the product's role clear.

## What to avoid

Generic inspirational soccer content with no Storelli-specific product
proof.

## Tone

Direct, practical, smart — not hypey.

## Claims discipline

Never overclaim injury prevention medically. Frame everything as protection,
risk reduction, or confidence — not a medical guarantee.
